🎯 Uso Geral

Use o verbo to be no passado para falar sobre situações que aconteceram no passado, como:

Estados e Sentimentos

"I was tired yesterday." (Eu estava cansada ontem.)

Localização

"She was at school." (Ela estava na escola.)

Idade no Passado

"I was 25 when I opened my company." (Eu tinha 25 anos quando abri a minha empresa.)

Características/Situações

"They were happy at the party." (Eles estavam felizes na festa.)

🧩 Formas (Estrutura)

✅ Affirmative

Sujeito + was / were
  • I / He / She / It ➔ WAS
  • You / We / They ➔ WERE

• I was at home.

• She was my teacher.

• We were friends.

• They were tired.

❌ Negative

Sujeito + was not / were not
  • was not ➔ wasn't
  • were not ➔ weren't

• I wasn't tired.

• He wasn't at school.

• We weren't ready.

• They weren't happy.

❓ Interrogative

Was / Were + Sujeito

Inverta a ordem para fazer perguntas.

• Was she at home?

• Were you busy yesterday?

• Was it difficult?

• Were they friends?

📖 Reading: A Busy Weekend

Last weekend, Julia was very busy. On Saturday morning, she was at the gym with her sister. They were very excited because it was their first fitness class. After that, Julia was at a restaurant with her family. The food was delicious, and everyone was happy.

In the afternoon, Julia and her cousins were at the shopping mall. The stores were crowded, but it was fun. At night, Julia was very tired, so she was at home. Her parents were tired too, and they were ready to sleep early.

On Sunday, Julia was sick, so she wasn’t at the park with her friends. She was in bed all day. The weekend wasn’t perfect, but it was still special.

Answer the questions:

  1. Where was Julia on Saturday morning?
  2. Who was with her?
  3. How was the food?
  4. Were the stores empty?
  5. Why was Julia at home at night?
  6. Was Julia at the park on Sunday?
  7. Where was she on Sunday?
  8. Was the weekend perfect?

Nota: Questões de texto podem ter várias formas corretas. Caso a sua resposta fique vermelha, mas ache que tem o mesmo sentido, verifique com a mentora.
